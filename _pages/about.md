<span class='anchor' id='about-me'></span>
I am currently studying at the School of Environmental Science and Engineering at Southern University of Science and Technology, under the guidance of [Researcher Zhenzhong Zeng](https://www.zhenzhongzeng.com/). I obtained my Master's degree from the Department of Remote Sensing and Geographic Information Engineering at Sun Yat-sen University, where I was fortunate to be mentored by [Academician Xinchang Zhang](http://www.sysuzxc.site/) and Professor [Qinchuan Xin](https://gp.sysu.edu.cn/zh-hans/teacher/187). I received my Bachelor's degree from the Department of Geographic Information Science at Hohai University's School of Earth Sciences and Engineering (now under the College of Hydrology and Water Resources), where I was guided by [Professor Ke Wang](https://jszy.hhu.edu.cn/wk100/). 

My research interests are extensive and include hydrology, vegetation remote sensing, Synthetic Aperture Radar quantitative remote sensing, disaster remote sensing, and big data and intelligent processing of remote sensing. By researching these topics, I hope to gain a deeper understanding of the complexities of our Earth and find new solutions for current environmental and resource management challenges. 

In terms of academic achievements, I have published more than 10 academic papers, with a total of <a href='https://scholar.google.com/citations?user=cvCQJg4AAAAJ'>157</a> citations. If you have any questions or would like to learn more about my research, please feel free to contact me. I look forward to collaborating with academic colleagues around the world.
